# Code of Conduct

Ariadne is a technical project. This document is short on purpose.

## Expectations

- Keep discussion technical, on-topic, and in good faith.
- Critique code, ideas, and decisions — not the people behind them.
- No harassment, personal attacks, doxxing, or spam.
- Disagree directly and move on; don't relitigate settled decisions.

## Enforcement

The maintainer has final say. Content or contributors that derail the project,
attack others, or make participation hostile may be edited, removed, or banned —
with or without warning, at the maintainer's discretion.

## Reporting

Report problems privately to `<OWNER-CONTACT-PLACEHOLDER>`. Reports are handled
confidentially.
